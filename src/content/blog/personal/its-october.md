It's October.

This is significant because it is my favorite time of year. In Central Texas, it's still not all that pleasant during the day, but we have had a few crisp mornings and the evenings are just begging for a walk around the neighborhood.

There is an annual pumpkin patch in my area, and I will be taking my engagement photos with my now betrothed on October 24th. Needless to say, I'm so excited for both of us to enjoy our favorite month. D&D is going to be soon, and I'm planning on making a [hard apple cider cheese dip](https://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/food-network-kitchen/pumpkin-hard-cider-cheese-dip-3364340) with pretzels for our spooky session.

We already pre-paid for the [Oktoberfest beer dinner at BJ's](https://www.bjsrestaurants.com/marketing-pages/beer-dinner), which involves a ton of food and Oktoberfest beer. Needless to say, the beer around here is phenomenal. And, of course, attending [Oktoberfest itself on the 4th](https://www.oktoberfestinfbg.com/), this Saturday.

This is not to mention the actual holiday of Halloween, my favorite holiday, and our annual re-watch of [Over The Garden Wall](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Over_the_Garden_Wall).

Happy spooky season 🎃
